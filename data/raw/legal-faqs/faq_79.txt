Q: 79. How is an Award passed by Permanent Lok Adalat?
A: One can request one’s legal aid lawyer to give update about the case status and the next date of hearing. Additionally, one may download the ‘e-courts’ app on mobile phone or visit their website here- https://services.ecourts.gov.in/ecourtindia_v6/ and enter the case details to find out all information related to the case.
