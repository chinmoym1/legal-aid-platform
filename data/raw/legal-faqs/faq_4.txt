Q: 4. What are the District Legal Services Authorities and the Taluka Legal Services Committees? What are their roles and responsibilities?
A: A State Legal Services Authority (SLSA) is constituted for every State/Union Territory under Section 6 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987. The SLSA is headed by the Chief Justice of the High Court of the respective State, as its Patron-in-Chief.
