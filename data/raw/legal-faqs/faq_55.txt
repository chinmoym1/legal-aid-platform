Q: 55.	Can one get free legal aid lawyer at any stage of case? Can one get free legal aid at the time of appeal even though a person had a personal lawyer before the appellate stage?
A: The grant of legal aid can be considered for filing a petition before the High Courts or Supreme Court against any interlocutory order including the rejection of bail. Such a person can get free Legal Aid if he/she satisfies the conditions and eligibility criteria as laid down under Section 12 of the Legal Services Authorities Act.
