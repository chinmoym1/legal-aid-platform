Q: 11. Are there any Helpline Services provided by NALSA for urgent legal assistance?
A: The Supreme Court Legal Services Committee (SCLSC) is constituted under Section 3A of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, to provide free and competent legal services to those who fall within the purview of Section 12 of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, in cases that fall under the jurisdiction of the Supreme Court of India.
