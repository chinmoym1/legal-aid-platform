Q: 73.	Is it compulsory for the parties to appear in person during a hearing in Permanent Lok Adalat for Public Utility Services?
A: Once the application is allowed, the applicant is given intimation about the assignment of a lawyer. A letter of appointment is also issued to the assigned lawyer with a copy to the applicant. The lawyer would thereafter contact the applicant at the earliest. The applicant may also contact the lawyer in the meanwhile.
