Q: 46.	Can Legal Services Institutions under the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, assist with matters related to family disputes and domestic violence?
A: As per Section 13(2) of the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, an affidavit made by a person as to his income is generally regarded as sufficient for making him/her eligible for the entitlement of legal services under the Act, unless the concerned Authority has reasons to question or disbelief such affidavit.
